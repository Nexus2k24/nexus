About:

Nexus 2024 is an initiative of Career guidance and counselling cell of CIT. It is launched by Carrier guidance and counselling cell, ECE Association, IETE Students' forum and IE(I) Students chapter. This is a nationwide initiative to provide students a solution-oriented platform for pressing problems in our everyday lives and thus create a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. It promotes innovation and out-of-the-box thinking in young minds especially engineering students across India.

Criteria for Judging:

INNOVATIVE IDEAS (30%)

Creativity & Innovation: How creative/innovative are the solutions proposed?

Practicality: How the tools/techniques proposed relevant in solving the problems?

BUSINESS FEASIBILITY (30%)

Market Potential: Does the solution address a significant market need or opportunity?

Revenue Model: Is the model plan for generating revenue realistic?

SOCIAL & ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT (20%)

Social and Environmental Impact: How does the solution contribute to positive social or environmental outcomes?

TEAM MANAGEMENT (10%)

Collaboration: How effectively did the team work together to develop the solution?

Roles and Responsibilities: Were team roles well-defined, and did team members contribute effectively?

PRESENTATION & COMMUNICATION (10%)

Clarity and effectiveness of the presentation. Ability to articulate and communicate the solution persuasively.

Career Guidance and Counselling Cell:

About:

Did You know?How a Career Guidance and Counseling Cell can not only shape your professional journey but also adapt to the dynamic landscape of the modern workforce?We, Career Guidance and Counseling Cell are typically a resource within CIT that provides support and guidance to individuals in making informed career choices. We offer assistance in exploring career options, understanding one's strengths and interests, and planning a career path. We often organize workshops, seminars, and one-on-one sessions to help individuals navigate the complexities of career decision-making. Our goal is to empower individuals to make well-informed choices aligned with their skills and aspirations.An interesting aspect of Career Guidance and Counseling Cells is our adaptation to emerging trends. In the digital age, some cells leverage technology, offering online assessments, virtual counseling sessions, and interactive platforms to enhance accessibility and reach a broader audience. This tech integration reflects a proactive approach to cater to the evolving needs of individuals seeking career guidance in a rapidly changing world.
